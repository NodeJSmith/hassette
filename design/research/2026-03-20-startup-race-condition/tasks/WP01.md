## Objectives & Success Criteria

- `run_forever()` starts only `DatabaseService` first, waits for it, creates the session, then starts all remaining children.
- `ready_event.set()` fires AFTER `create_session()` completes, so BusService and SchedulerService cannot process events until the session exists.
- `_start_resources()` is replaced with a two-phase approach: `_start_database()` + `_start_remaining_resources()`.
- Existing test `test_run_forever_starts_and_shuts_down` passes with updated assertions verifying phased ordering.
- `test_run_forever_handles_session_init_failure` continues to pass.

## Subtasks

1. In `src/hassette/core/core.py`, replace `_start_resources()` with two methods:
   - `_start_database()` ‚Äî calls `self._database_service.start()` only
   - `_start_remaining_resources()` ‚Äî iterates `self.children`, calls `service.start()` for every child except `self._database_service`
2. Rewrite `run_forever()` startup sequence:
   - Call `_start_database()`
   - `await self.wait_for_ready([self.database_service], timeout=...)`
   - `await self._session_manager.mark_orphaned_sessions()`
   - `await self._session_manager.create_session()`
   - Call `_start_remaining_resources()`
   - Log "Waiting for resources to initialize..."
   - `self.ready_event.set()`
   - `await self.wait_for_ready(list(self.children), timeout=...)`
   - (rest unchanged)
3. Remove the earlier partial fix's separate `wait_for_ready([self.database_service])` call that currently sits between `_start_resources()` and `wait_for_ready(all)` ‚Äî it's now integrated into the phased startup.
4. Update `tests/integration/test_core.py::test_run_forever_starts_and_shuts_down`:
   - Mock `_start_database` and `_start_remaining_resources` instead of `_start_resources`
   - Verify `_start_database` is called first
   - Verify `create_session` is called before `_start_remaining_resources`
   - Verify `wait_for_ready` is called for DB service before remaining resources
5. Update `tests/integration/test_core.py::test_run_forever_handles_session_init_failure` ‚Äî replace `_start_resources` mock with `_start_database` mock.
