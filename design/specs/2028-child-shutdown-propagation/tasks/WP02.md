## Objectives & Success Criteria

- `Resource._finalize_shutdown()` automatically calls `shutdown()` on all children in reverse insertion order after `cleanup()` and before `handle_stop()`.
- Child shutdown uses `asyncio.gather(return_exceptions=True)` with a `resource_shutdown_timeout_seconds` timeout.
- Each child shutdown failure is logged with the child's `unique_name`; exceptions do not propagate out.
- Children list is snapshot via `list(reversed(self.children))` before the gather to guard against concurrent mutation.
- The `event_streams_closed` guard on `handle_stop()` is preserved.
- `Service._finalize_shutdown()` inherits propagation from `Resource` (no override needed).
- Hassette's `on_shutdown()` pre-shuts-down children; `_shutdown_completed` flag (WP01) makes the subsequent propagation a no-op.

## Subtasks

1. In `Resource._finalize_shutdown()` (`src/hassette/resources/base.py:244`), after the `cleanup()` call and before the `event_streams_closed` / `handle_stop()` block, add:
   - Snapshot children: `children = list(reversed(self.children))`
   - If children non-empty, gather shutdown with timeout and log exceptions
   - Set `self._shutdown_completed = True` after child propagation, before `handle_stop()`
2. Write unit tests in `tests/unit/resources/test_lifecycle_propagation.py`:
   - `test_shutdown_propagates_to_children_in_reverse_order` — parent with 3 children, verify shutdown order is reverse insertion.
   - `test_shutdown_propagation_error_tolerance` — middle child raises in `on_shutdown`, verify other children still shut down.
   - `test_shutdown_propagation_logs_child_errors` — verify error is logged with child `unique_name`.
   - `test_shutdown_propagation_skips_completed_children` — pre-shutdown a child, then shutdown parent, verify child's `on_shutdown` runs only once (uses `_shutdown_completed` from WP01).
   - `test_shutdown_propagation_with_no_children` — leaf Resource shuts down normally.
   - `test_service_inherits_shutdown_propagation` — Service subclass with children, verify propagation runs after serve task cancellation.
