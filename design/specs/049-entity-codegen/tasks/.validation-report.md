## Step 4: Gaps and Problems

### Gap #1 — FR#1 misattributed: extraction and generation capability not formally claimed

**Severity:** Coverage Gap

**FR#1 text:** "The tool extracts IntFlag feature enums from HA core component `const.py` and `__init__.py` files and generates equivalent Python enums colocated with their domain's state model."

FR#1 has two distinct parts: (a) **extraction** — reading IntFlag enums from HA core source, and (b) **generation** — producing colocated Python enums in domain state files.

T01 is the only task that claims FR#1 in its `implements` list. But T01 is a prerequisite migration task: it manually dissolves the existing hand-written `features.py` by moving enums into domain files. Its Verify item reads: "IntFlag enums are defined in their domain state files, not in a separate features.py." This verifies the migration outcome, not the generator's capability.

The actual implementation of FR#1's extraction part is in T04 (which builds `extractors/features.py`) and the generation part is in T07 (which renders enums colocated in `state_model.py.j2`). Neither T04 nor T07 lists FR#1 in their `implements` array, and neither has a Verify item for it.

**Impact:** An implementer walking T04 and T07 will not know FR#1 is their responsibility. The Verify checklists for T04 and T07 do not include a binary-verifiable check that the generator correctly extracts and generates IntFlag enums.

**Fix:** Add `FR#1` to T04's `implements` list with a Verify item such as: "FR#1: `extractors/features.py` extracts IntFlag enums from `const.py` AND `__init__.py` — verified by fan (IntFlag in `__init__.py`) and light (IntFlag in `const.py`) both producing correct enum members." Also add `FR#1` to T07's `implements` list with a Verify item confirming the colocated enum appears in the rendered state model file.

### Gap #2 — AC#6 real verification missing from T12's Verify section

**Severity:** Coverage Gap

**AC#6 text:** "The tool completes generation for all targeted domains in under 30 seconds on a local checkout (FR#8)."

T10 claims AC#6 in its `implements` list with Verify item: "Full generation completes in under 30 seconds on a local checkout." However, T10's unit tests use mocked extractors. T10's own Focus note acknowledges: "Performance target: under 30 seconds for all 30 domains on a local checkout. AST parsing is fast; bottleneck is ruff formatting."

T12 contains the actual performance test against real HA core ("Time the full generation of all 30 domains — Assert < 30 seconds") and explicitly notes: "AC#6 — tested here since T10's unit test mocks are fast." But AC#6 does not appear in T12's `implements` list and is absent from T12's Verify section. The real measurement of AC#6 lives in T12's prose but is not a checkable item.

**Impact:** T10's Verify item for AC#6 can pass (mocked components are always fast) while the actual constraint — real extraction + ruff formatting across 30 domains under 30 seconds — goes unverified through the formal checklist.

**Fix:** Add `AC#6` to T12's `implements` list and add a Verify item: "AC#6: Full generation of all targeted domains against real HA core at `~/source/core` completes in under 30 seconds (measured by integration test timer assertion)."
