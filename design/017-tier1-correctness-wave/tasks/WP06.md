## Objectives & Success Criteria

- `hassette.prod.toml` and `hassette.dev.toml` contain ONLY the 19 fields that differ between environments
- All 27 redundant entries (identical in both files) are removed
- Setting `HASSETTE__LOG_LEVEL=DEBUG` with no service-specific overrides causes ALL 12 service log level fields to resolve to `"DEBUG"`
- `AppConfig.log_level` also correctly inherits the global log level
- Unit test proves the propagation works
